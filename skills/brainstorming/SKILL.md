> Adapted from [obra/superpowers](https://github.com/obra/superpowers) (MIT License).

This skill provides a structured protocol for turning ideas into validated designs before any code is written.

To execute a brainstorming session, you must follow the protocol defined in the reference prompt:

1. **Read the Prompt:** Load `references/brainstorming-prompt.md` into your context.
2. **Execute:** Follow its phases precisely, conducting a collaborative design dialogue.
3. **Output:** Produce a design document in `docs/plans/YYYY-MM-DD-<topic>-design.md`.
